Mella is an [original character](/original-character/) I created for RP in ESO.
She's participated in multiple settings but there are a few principles I keep
to regardless of the setting.

## Behavioral Traits

Regardless of the setting, Mella always has the following behavioral traits:

* profoundly energetic
* empathetic
* impulsive

## Main Background Story Beats

* Mella is a werewolf or the equivelent in that setting
* She ran away from home at some point in her past (details can depend on the
   setting).
