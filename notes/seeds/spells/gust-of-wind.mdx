date: 2022-03-27

### Description

A line of strong wind about 60 feet (~20 meters) long and 10 feet (~2.5 meters)
wide blasts away from the character. People and creatures caught within can be
staggered or even pushed back by the force of the blast.

The wind can be used to dissipate a gas or vapor, extinguish candles or torches,
or make a large fire flare up with additional oxygen.

### Stats

**School:** Destruction

**Skill Level:** Apprentice
